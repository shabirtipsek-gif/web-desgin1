Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the project price?

The project price includes design, development, testing, and deployment. Additional services like hosting, domain registration, and ongoing maintenance are available as add-ons.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Starter packages typically take 2-3 weeks, Professional packages 3-4 weeks, and Enterprise solutions 4-6 weeks.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All packages include technical support for a specified period. Extended support and maintenance plans are available after the initial support period ends.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your package at any time. We'll apply any payments you've already made toward your new plan.
