My Story

My journey into technology began with a simple curiosity about how websites work. What started as tinkering with HTML and CSS has evolved into a passion for creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional.

I believe that good design is not just about aesthetics - it's about solving problems and creating intuitive experiences for users. This philosophy guides my work in both web development and UI/UX design.

Beyond Code

When I'm not coding, you'll find me in the kitchen experimenting with flavors and techniques. Cooking is my creative outlet and a way to connect with different cultures through food.

My recipe book is a collection of dishes that tell stories - from family traditions to culinary adventures and everything in between.
